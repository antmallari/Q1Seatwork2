Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Image of Characters Name of Characters Description of Characters
Maria Clara Maria Clara is a devout Roman Catholic who became the epitome of virtue, "demure and self-effacing," humorless and prone to fainting, with a "Oriental decoration" with "downcast" eyes and a "pure soul."
Don Rafael Ibarra Ibarra was a young man who was sincere and idealistic. He sought to improve the country, influenced by his European education; as part of this, he believed in the power of education to enact reforms and worked to establish a school in San Diego to that end.
Teniente Guevarra Teniente Guevarra, full name Miguel Guevarra, was an elderly Guardia Civil lieutenant and close friend of Don Rafael Ibarra. With a deep respect for the man, he later worked to protect Don Rafael's son Crisostomo after he returned from Europe.
